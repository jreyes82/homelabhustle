Tools I Actually Use

Everything listed here is running in my homelab right now or is something I actively recommend. No fluff, no tools I haven't touched.

Media Stack
P
Plex

Personal media server. Stream your own movies, TV, and music from anywhere. The backbone of my media setup.

Free + paid pass
S
Sonarr

Automatic TV show management. Monitors, grabs, and organizes episodes into your library automatically.

Free
R
Radarr

Same as Sonarr but for movies. Set it and forget it — movies get grabbed and organized automatically.

Free
L
Lidarr

Automatic music collection management. Monitors artists and grabs new releases in your preferred format.

Free
A
Aurral

Self-hosted music streaming frontend that connects to your Lidarr library. Clean UI for browsing and playing your collection.

Free
S
SABnzbd

Usenet download client. Works alongside Sonarr and Radarr to handle the actual downloading.

Free
O
Overseerr

Request management for Plex. Let friends and family request movies and shows through a clean interface.

Free
T
Tautulli

Plex monitoring and analytics. See who's watching what, track history, and get notifications.

Free

DNS, tunnels, Zero Trust access, and DDoS protection. I use it for everything domain-related on this site and in my homelab.

Free tier
